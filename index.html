Studying Computer Science and Business at Lehigh University

Lehigh's program in Computer Science and Business (CSB) is a joint initiative between the College of Business and Economics and the Department of Computer Science and Engineering in the P.C. Rossin College of Engineering and Applied Science. Students completing this demanding program are awarded a unique joint degree from both colleges. It equips students with the high-tech and business skills they need at a time when the boundaries between the two fields are becoming increasingly blurred.
